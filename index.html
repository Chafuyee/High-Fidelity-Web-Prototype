Who We Are

We are a passionate team of scientists, staff, and members dedicated to advancing conservation efforts in over 70 countries, committed to making a lasting impact.

Our collective mission is to protect and preserve the rich biodiversity of our planet, ensuring the well-being of both wildlife and ecosystems for generations to come. Through extensive research, innovative initiatives, and strategic collaborations, we strive to address the pressing environmental challenges that threaten our planet. Our team of experts combines scientific expertise with practical solutions to develop and implement conservation strategies that make a tangible difference on the ground.

What We Do

To maximize our impact on the climate and biodiversity crises by 2030, we prioritize breakthrough ideas, empower local leaders, and influence policy at all levels.

Central to our approach is the recognition that our individual and collective actions contribute to the larger environmental picture. As part of our commitment to transparency and accountability, we have developed a powerful tool: our team's carbon footprint calculator. This innovative tool enables individuals, organizations, and communities to assess and understand their carbon emissions, empowering them to make informed choices and take effective action to reduce their environmental impact.

How You Can Help

There are indeed numerous ways to make a positive change for our planet, and we wholeheartedly invite you to join us in our efforts.

Our commitment to reducing carbon footprints aligns with our overarching goal of mitigating climate change. By engaging with our carbon footprint calculator, you can gain valuable insights into the environmental impact of your daily activities and make informed choices to reduce your carbon emissions. The calculator will guide you through a comprehensive assessment, highlighting areas where you can make significant changes, such as adopting energy-efficient practices, reducing waste, and embracing sustainable transportation options. Together, we can make a substantial collective impact by taking steps towards a low-carbon lifestyle.
